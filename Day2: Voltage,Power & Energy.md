# Voltage
Voltage is the potential difference between two points that causes current to flow.  
(or) Voltage is energy (work done) per unit charge.

**Formula:**
V = W/Q  
V = IR  

**SI Unit:** Volt (V)

---

# Power
Power is the amount of energy transferred per unit time.

**Formula:**
P = VI  
P = V^2 / R  
P = I^2 R  

**SI Unit:** Watt (W)

---

# Energy
Energy is the ability to do work.

**Formula:**
E = Pt  
(or) E = VIt  

**SI Unit:** Joule (J)

---

# Types of Energy

1. **Kinetic Energy** – Energy due to motion  
   KE = 1/2 mv^2  

2. **Potential Energy** – Energy due to stored position  
   PE = mgh
