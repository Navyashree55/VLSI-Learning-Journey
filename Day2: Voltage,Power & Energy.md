# Voltage
Voltage is the potential difference between two points that causes current to flow.
          (or)
Voltage is Energy(workdone) per unit charge. 

**Formula :**
        V = W/Q     
        V=IR
**SI Unit :** Volt(V)

# Power
Power is the amount of energy transfered per unit time.
**Formula :**
        P =VI  
        P = V^2/R 
        P= I^2 R
**SI unit** - Watt(W)

# Energy
Energy is the ability to do work.

**Formula :** 
      E = Pt  (or)  E= V I t
**SI unit -** Joule(J)

**Types of Energy**
1. Kinetic energy - Energy due to motion
   KE = 1/2 mv^2
2. Potential Energy - Energy due to stored position
   PE = mgh
