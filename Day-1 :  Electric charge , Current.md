# Electric Charge

Electric charge is the property of particles which causes them to experience electric forces such as (attraction and repulsion).

**Types**
1. Protons (positive charge)
2. electrons (negative charge)
   
   **Formula:**
         Q= It
   
   **SI Unit -** Coloumb [C]
   
**Note:** 1. Like charges repel
       2. Unlike charges attract

# Current
Electric current is the rate of flow of electric charges.

**Formula :**
        I = Q/t
        
**SI unit -** Amperes [A]
