# Inductor

## Definition
- An inductor is a passive component that stores energy in a magnetic field.

## Unit
- Henry (H)

---

## Basic Formula
V = L (dI/dt)

---

## Energy Stored
E = 1/2 × L × I²

---

## Working
- Consists of a coil of wire
- Stores energy when current flows

---

## Behavior

- Opposes change in current

### DC:
- Initially opposes current
- Acts as short circuit in steady state

### AC:
- Opposes AC

---

## Inductive Reactance
Xₗ = 2πfL

- Higher frequency → higher reactance

---

## Applications
- Filters
- Transformers
- Energy storage

---

## Key Insight
- Inductor stores energy in magnetic field
- Opposes change in current
